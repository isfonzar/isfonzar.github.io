## What is DDD and why use it?

Domain-Driven Design (DDD) is a set of tools and patterns to assist in [designing](#Why design?) and implementing software that delivers high value. The aim of the book is to distill the DDD approach in a way that's easy to pick up and learn.

### Why design?

Design exists wether it's intentional or not. There is no such thing as "no design", only bad design. So design matters.
